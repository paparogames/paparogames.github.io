Stillness is your only cover

You're a tiny inchworm in an apple grove, and a hungry snake is hunting. When you move, the snake can see you. When you hold still, you fade out of sight. But you can't wait forever — seeds expire, the exit won't open itself, and the snake gets faster every level. Every single step is a decision.
