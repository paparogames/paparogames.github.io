Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how paparogames ("we") handles information in the game Inchworm, Stay Still ("the game"). We keep this short because the game collects very little.

What the game stores

Your game progress — coins, unlocked skins, best level and score — is stored only on your own device. It is never uploaded to us and never leaves your phone unless you back it up yourself through your device or Google account.

Analytics

The game uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand, in aggregate, how the game is played (for example, how often a level is completed). This helps us balance and improve the game. This data is anonymous and not used to identify you personally. Firebase is provided by Google; see Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

What we do NOT do

  • No ads.
  • No in-app purchases.
  • No account or sign-in required.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children in violation of applicable law.

Permissions

The game may request vibration access to provide haptic feedback. It does not require access to your contacts, location, camera, or files.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at paparogames@gmail.com.
